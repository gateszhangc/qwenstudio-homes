FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Quick answers to the most common questions about Qwen Studio.

What is Qwen Studio?

Qwen Studio is the official web-based chat interface for the Qwen family of large language models developed by Alibaba Cloud. It provides free access to powerful AI chat capabilities directly in your browser.

Is Qwen Studio free to use?

Yes. The basic Qwen Studio chat experience is completely free. There are no paywalls, subscriptions, or credit card requirements for standard use.

What models does Qwen Studio use?

Qwen Studio runs on the latest Qwen series models, including Qwen2.5 and Qwen3.x iterations. These models deliver competitive performance across reasoning, coding, mathematics, and multilingual tasks.

Can I upload files to Qwen Studio?

Yes. Qwen Studio supports file uploads including images, PDFs, Word documents, Excel files, and plain text files. The AI can read and analyze the content of uploaded documents.

Does Qwen Studio support multiple languages?

Yes. Qwen models are trained on multilingual data and can understand and generate content in English, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, French, German, Spanish, and many other languages.

How does Qwen Studio compare to other AI chat platforms?

Qwen Studio offers competitive performance with strong reasoning and coding abilities, a generous free tier, file upload support, and a long context window — making it a solid alternative to other major AI chat platforms.
